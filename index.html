Peter's Pizza Panic Pandomonium!

Deliver the pizza pies to passers by to pick up points for the Peter Parker guy!


How to Play

Help Peter deliver all the pizzas to all the hungry Queens before time runs out.

Controls
W = Move Peter Parker (UP) a lane.
A = Make Peter Parker (SLOWDOWN).
S = Move Peter Parker (DOWN) a lane.
D = Make Peter Parker (SPEED UP).
