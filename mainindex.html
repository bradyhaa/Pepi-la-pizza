Peter's Pizza Panic Pandomonium!

How to Play

Help Peter deliver as many pizza pies to the people of St. Petersburg before rush hour hits his bike.

Keep an eye out for any wild yellow cabs, for the ride of a delivery bike can be a perillous one.
